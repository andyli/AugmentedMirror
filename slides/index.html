Air Drum

System overview

Align the Real and the Virtual

  • naive (wrong) method:
    1. put a virtual camera at the head position
    2. project virtual objects on the mirror plane

When the head is off-axis...

  • It is actually 99% of the case because user moves.
  • Visually even worse when screen is big.

Generalized Perspective Projection

http://csc.lsu.edu/~kooima/misc.html
  • correct (single point) projection
  • update head position with head tracking
  • =>motion parallax
  • very "3D" when viewing with single eye

Tracking Drum Sticks

  • the stick is hard to track in front view:
  • the depth varies too much =>
    hard to segment depth image
  • may become a tiny dot when pointing at Kinect

Put Kinect on top of user

  • silhouette is more stable
  • find stick by looking at the contour
  • bonus: closest area is head
